# Gemtale
Gemtale is a lightweight LLM synthesizer that showcases the ScySDK. It also gives researchers a tool to combine their interests in an interdisciplinary way. The focus is to get people outside the field of quantum mechanics to learn just how they can engage their own interests with a quantum-orientation.
