# ScyWeb References

This folder serves as a centralized repository for the foundational academic research, technical reports, and historical texts that inform the development of **ScyWeb**. The materials here provide the theoretical basis for our implementations of fractal-based spatial scrambling, chaotic diffusion, and quantum-resistant data architecture. The materials are not complete and in many cases just represent previews to prove their existence.

### 📖 About these References

The documents in this folder represent the core pillars of the ScyWeb methodology:
* **Geometric Stream Ciphers:** Foundations of the Morton-Z order and Hilbert curves for spatial de-correlation.
* **Analog-to-Digital Transition:** Theoretical models for destroying local gradients and image textures.
* **Post-Quantum Readiness:** Analysis of quantum algorithms (Shor's, Grover's) and the necessity for quantum-resistant obfuscation.
* **Cryptographic History:** From the Spartan Skytale to modern block cipher protocols.
